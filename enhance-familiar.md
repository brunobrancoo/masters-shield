Enhance familiar

Casting time: 1 action
Range: touch
Components: V, S, M (a fur, fether or scale from you familiar and salamander newt) 
Duration: 1 hour 

You perform an ancient ritual to empower your familiar, causing its form to change to a magically enhanced beast. Your familiar gains the following benefits for the duration:

[  ] Your familiar gains a number of temporary hit points equal to 10x the spell level. If it loses all its temp hp the spell ends.
[  ] Your familiar size becomes large
[  ] Your familiar strength and constitution are calculated by your spell casting hability
[  ] your familiar to hit bonus becomes your spell casting to hit bonus and save DC for your spells (for ex the pseudodragon Sting hability)
[  ] Your familiar gains multiattack and the die used in their attack is increased by one size
[  ] Your familiar can use it reaction to take a hit for you if you are with in 10 feet of it, when it does so you two split the damage. It can use this hability a number of times equal to prof bonus

If the spell is upcasted in 8th or 9th level your familiar attacks 3 times with its multiattack hability.
